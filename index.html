Product Line

KardiaMobile 1-Lead - $79

FDA cleared 2012. Consumer screening device. Uses ultrasonic audio encoding (~18kHz) transmitted to phone microphone. Instant AFib/Normal results.

KardiaMobile 6L - $99-129

FDA cleared 2019. 30s recording, 500Hz sample rate, 24-bit resolution, ~21g weight. Detects AFib + 10+ arrhythmias via KAI AI.

KardiaMobile Card - ~$99

FDA cleared Nov 2021. Credit card form factor. Bluetooth connection, Lead I ECG, instant AI analysis.

KardiaMobile 12L - $3,000+

FDA cleared June 2024. Clinician-grade, hospital-quality 12-lead ECG. Breakthrough: 5 electrodes vs traditional 10 (50% reduction). Single-cable design.

How It Works

AI chatbot handles prescription refills by asking questions. Only handles refills (not new prescriptions). Escalates to doctor when uncertain.

Validation

Tested on 500 urgent care cases with AI and real physicians reviewing independently. AI matched physician decisions 99.2% of the time. This evidence was submitted to and accepted by Utah regulators.
